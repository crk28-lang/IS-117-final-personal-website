An Introduction to Swedish Cottage Design

Swedish cottage style, or "stuga" in Swedish, represents one of the most influential design movements in Scandinavian history. Rooted in practical necessity and refined through centuries of cultural refinement, this aesthetic has shaped interiors, furniture design, and architectural principles worldwide.

Historical Roots

Originating in rural Sweden during the 17th and 18th centuries, Swedish cottage style emerged from the need for functional, durable design in harsh northern climates.

Design Principles

Built on simplicity, functionality, and harmony with nature, Swedish cottage style emphasizes clean lines, natural materials, and a refined color palette.
